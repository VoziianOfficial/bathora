FAQ

Common questions before comparing bathroom providers.

How do I compare local bathroom providers?

Start by choosing the project type, checking service availability, reviewing quote details, and verifying license and insurance before hiring any provider.

Does Bathora perform bathroom work directly?

No. Bathora is an independent matching platform. Providers are separate companies, and homeowners choose who they want to hire.

Are provider quotes usually free?

Many providers may offer free quotes, but policies can vary by company and project type. Confirm quote terms directly with the provider.

What affects bathroom project pricing?

Layout, materials, plumbing needs, fixture choices, room size, timeline, and local labor availability can all affect pricing.
