Aggregator note

Service Type

Vanity projects can change the look and function of a bathroom.

A vanity update may include cabinet replacement, sink changes, countertop selection, mirror placement, lighting coordination, or storage improvements.

Bathora helps homeowners compare provider options based on project scope, measurements, fixture needs, and local availability.

Provider Comparison

What to compare before choosing a vanity provider.

Vanity work can depend on measurements, plumbing location, countertop material, sink type, and installation scope.

Vanity size

Single, double, floating, freestanding, custom, or compact bathroom vanity options.

Sink layout

Plumbing position, faucet type, drain placement, and countertop compatibility.

Finish level

Cabinet finish, countertop material, hardware, mirror style, and lighting coordination.

Provider terms

Quote details, installation scope, license, insurance, timeline, and warranty language.
