Service Type

Bathroom surfaces need the right material and provider fit.

Tile and flooring projects may involve floor replacement, wall tile, shower tile, waterproofing details, grout, subfloor review, or surface preparation.

Bathora helps homeowners compare provider options based on surface type, room conditions, material choices, and project scope.

Provider Comparison

What to compare for tile and flooring providers.

Surface projects depend on prep work, waterproofing needs, layout, tile material, room size, and installation details.

Tile type

Porcelain, ceramic, stone, mosaic, large-format tile, or floor-focused materials.

Surface prep

Subfloor condition, waterproofing, leveling, wall preparation, and demolition needs.

Layout details

Pattern, grout lines, transitions, shower walls, niches, corners, and finish edges.

Quote clarity

Material inclusion, labor scope, timeline, warranty, license, and insurance verification.
